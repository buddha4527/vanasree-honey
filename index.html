About the Honey

Pure Forest Honey is collected from wild bee hives found in unpolluted forest areas. Harvested using traditional and sustainable methods, it ensures minimal disturbance to bee colonies and forest ecosystems.

This honey is raw, unheated and minimally filtered, preserving its natural flavour, aroma and nutritional properties. Each batch reflects the richness of forest flora.

Why Choose Forest Honey

  • No artificial processing or additives
  • Collected from deep forest regions
